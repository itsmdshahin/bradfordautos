FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How long will my car repair take?+
Smart and minor repairs are often completed the same day. Panel repairs and full resprays typically take 3–5 working days. We'll give you an accurate timeframe when you book.
Do you work with insurance companies?+
Yes — we're approved by major UK insurers and can liaise directly with your insurer on your behalf, making the whole claims process completely stress-free for you.
Will the paint match perfectly?+
Absolutely. We use computerised colour-matching technology to blend paint seamlessly with your vehicle's existing finish. Most customers genuinely cannot tell which panel was repaired.
Is there a warranty on repairs?+
All our workmanship is covered by a written guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll put it right — at no extra cost to you. We stand behind everything we do.
Where are you located?+
We're based at Unit D(1), Annison Street, Bradford, BD3 9H — easy to reach with on-site parking. We're open Mon–Fri 8am–6pm and Saturday 9am–4pm.
Do I need to book in advance?+
For best results we recommend booking ahead, though we do our best to accommodate walk-ins and same-day smart repairs when capacity allows. Call or WhatsApp us to check availability.
